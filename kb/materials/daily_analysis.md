# Material Analysis: Daily English

## What it is
- 1305 podcast episodes with large transcript PDFs.
- Average audio length ~18 minutes.

## Pedagogical value
- Controlled, clear speech for decoding foundation.
- Strong for high-frequency daily life domains.
- Good for precision listening and recap training.

## Risks
- If used alone, transfer to authentic YouTube can be weak.

## Recommended role in curriculum
- Early and mid-stage backbone for accuracy building.
- Use in 30-min lesson as one precision segment (not whole lesson).

## Open tasks
- Build episode-level topic ladder and lexical burden tags.
- Mark best episodes for "first-pass no pause" drills.
